Accident-related
Insurance

Accident-related insurance provides
financial protection for accidental injuries,
covering a wide range of expenses and
safeguarding individuals from potential
financial burdens.
Accident-related insurance covers:

  • Medical expenses for
    accidental injuries
  • Emergency care costs
  • Disability benefits for lost income
    due to accident-related disability
  • Accidental Death and Dismemberment
    (AD&D) coverage
  • Rehabilitation services
